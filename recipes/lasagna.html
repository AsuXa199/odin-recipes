Lasagna

Description

This is a very good recipe for making delicious lasagna. It's very easy and desn't need a lot of ingredients

Ingredients

  • Lasagna
  • Pasta Sauce
  • Garlic
  • Beef and Sausage
  • Tomato
  • Egg
  • Parsley
  • Onion
  • Parmesan

Steps

  1. Boil pasta
  2. Prepare meat sauce
  3. Combine cheese mixture
  4. Layer & bake
  5. Let it rest at least 15 minutes before cutting
