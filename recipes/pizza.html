Pizza

Description

This is a very good recipe for a delicious home-made pizza. It's very easy and doesn't need a lot of ingredients

Ingredients

  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • Cornmeal (to help slide the pizza onto the pizza stone)
  • Tomato sauce (smooth or pureed)
  • Firm mozzarella cheese, grated
  • Fresh soft mozzarella cheese, separated into small clumps
  • Fontina cheese, grated
  • Parmesan cheese, grated
  • Feta cheese, crumbled

Steps

  1. Proof the yeast
  2. Make and knead the pizza dough
  3. Let the dough rise
  4. Divide the dough into 2 balls
  5. Prep the toppings
  6. Flatten the dough ball, and stretch out into a round
